About

Yella is a multifaceted individual who grew up in Essex and East London, UK.

Over the years, she has cultivated a range of diverse interests and talents, including skateboarding for 3 years, Martial arts in kickboxing and Brazilian jiu-jitsu for a total of 6 years, modelling, pole dancing, yoga and coding while also establishing successful skateboarding and modelling collaborations with more than 20 prominent brands including NikeSB, Adidas, Google UK, Dove, Primark, Disney, Virgin Media, Puma, Manchester United FC, End Clothing, Butter Goods, Surfdome and Brixton Brewery.

Yella harnesses her unique strengths to fuel her passion for learning, creativity, and exploring new avenues of interest.
